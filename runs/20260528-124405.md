## What surprised me
- The user-suspected modern slogan turns out to have a real Pāḷi canonical seed (AN 8.8 Uttaravipattisutta with grain-heap simile) AND a principled Mahāyāna development (Adhyāśayasaṃcodanasūtra with four content-criteria); the corruption is only at step 3, when the formula is detached from criteria.
- The DN-Aṭṭhakathā explicitly fuses the mahāpadesa (textual test) with the Gotamī criteria (functional test) — citing AN 8.53 verbatim as the gloss on *vinaya* in the mahāpadesa. Strong commentarial support for the disciple's-criterion reading.
- `resolve-citation` mis-resolved Snp verse-number 452 to "SNP28 Padhānasuttaṃ" because Suttanipāta numbering is global verse-numbers, not sutta-local. Worth noting in the helper docs.

## What I'd change in the skill
- Document the Suttanipāta paranum quirk in SKILL.md's resolve-citation guidance: in Snp, `paranum` is a verse number across the whole nikāya, not a sutta-local paragraph. Recommend citing by sutta name + verse range when working in Snp.
