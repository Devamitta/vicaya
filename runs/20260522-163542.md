## What surprised me
- The fire simile (MN72) maps perfectly onto the immanent/transcendent polarity — the fire burning right now (immanent: the conditioned aggregates) vs. the fire going out without direction (transcendent: unconditioned) — this wasn't obvious in advance but is the best canonical simile for this topic.
- The `asaṅkhat` stem search in Khuddaka returned useful hits across Theragāthā, Vimānavatthu, Apadāna — the term is pervasive, not just in the Udāna.
