## What surprised me
- *Kukkucca* is classified in the Abhidhamma with *dosa* (Dhs §1503), not with *uddhacca* — they arise in different cittas. The famous compound is asymmetric.
- The Paṭisambhidāmagga (Paṭis I.31, 103, 163) actually omits *kukkucca* from its hindrance list, substituting *arati* and *avijjā*. The hindrance enumeration is canonically variant.
- The Sarvāstivāda treats *kaukṛtya* as ethically indeterminate — can be wholesome regret. Theravāda treats it as always unwholesome. Same word, different valence.
